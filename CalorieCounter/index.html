The purpose of this app is to track the total caloric value of the meals/food you've eaten in a day. You can enter as many types of food/meals as you like, one at a time, and the website will remember your input if you close the browser/tab. This app assumes you already know the amount of calories the food you input is worth and does not account for portion sizes.
