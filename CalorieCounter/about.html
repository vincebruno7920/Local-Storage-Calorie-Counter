Thanks for showing interest in my little web-app. Enter as many types of meals and the calories they contain and this site will calculate the total number of calories you've input on the bottom where it says Total Calories.

You can use this site to track the amount of calories you've had in a day by using your local storage to store the info you typed in. You can close the tab/browser and it will still remember the info you input. If you wish to start over, simply click the Clear button to erase the data you've already input. This will clear the data you've stored on your local storage device/browser for this website and effectively reset your input. Max storage is dependent on your web browser/machine (about 5MB).
